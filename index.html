About

This calculator uses table_1 to correct proof readings based on temperature. Table_1 works with hydrometers that are calibrated at 60 degrees Fahrenheit and can handle most readings between 0 and 200 proof; from 1F to 100F. It is somewhat limited at very high and very low temperatures. You can find more information on this method in the TTB Gauging Manual. You can view the source code for this calculator and download a typed version of table_1 here. I hand-typed this table and can't guarantee 100% accuracy. Please let me know if you find a mistake.
