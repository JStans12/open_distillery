This calculator uses table_1 to correct proof readings based on temperature. Table_1 works with hydrometers that are calibrated at 60 degrees Fahrenheit and can handle most readings between 0 and 200 proof; from 1°F to 100°F. It is somewhat limited at very high and very low temperatures. You can find more information on this method in the TTB GAUGING MANUAL. You can download a typed version of table_1 HERE. I hand-typed this table and can't guarantee 100% accuracy. Please let me know if you find a mistake.
